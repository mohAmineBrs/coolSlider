The name Stella is of Latin and Italian origin meaning “celestial star.” In the past, it could also have been used to describe someone who lived in a location with the word "star" in its name.

Helena as a girl's name is of Greek origin, and the meaning of Helena is "shining light". Helena is a variant of the Greek names Eleanor and Helen. Place name: Helena is the state capital of Montana.
